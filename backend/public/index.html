What is Plurihive

Plurihive turns outdoor activity into a live map game. Every run captures territory, fuels your progress, and pushes you up the rankings.

Live territory capture

Your exact GPS path becomes your territory on the map. Defend, expand, and claim more ground with every run.

Power drops

Collect power drops placed on public roads to boost points, streaks, and mission progress.

Live leaderboards

Track your rank in real time across your city and globally as you capture more territory.

Run recap

Finish strong with a detailed recap: distance, pace, splits, and shareable moments.

Capture territory live
