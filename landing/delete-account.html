Delete Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to delete your Plurihive account.

Delete in the App

You can delete your account directly in the app. This removes your profile and activity data from our servers.

  1. Open the Plurihive app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger zone.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Need Help?

If you cannot access the app, email support@territoryfitness.com with the subject "Account Deletion" and we will verify your identity and process the request.
