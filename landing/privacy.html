Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your data.

Last updated: January 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Plurihive ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the Plurihive mobile app and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account data such as name, email address, and authentication identifiers.
  • Location data (precise, in the background when tracking) to draw routes, capture territories, and show nearby activity.
  • Health and fitness data such as steps, distance, calories, and heart rate from Health Connect or Google Fit if you grant access.
  • Activity data including route points, timestamps, workouts, territories captured, and game progress.
  • Device and app data like device model, OS version, app version, and diagnostic logs to improve stability.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core gameplay and tracking features.
  • Calculate stats, leaderboards, and rewards.
  • Sync data across devices and restore progress.
  • Send notifications you enable in settings.
  • Improve performance, security, and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal or health data. We only share data with service providers that help operate the app (hosting and analytics), and only as required to deliver the service.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account in the app. When you request deletion, we remove your account and associated activity data, unless retention is required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Security

We use encryption in transit and standard security practices to help protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

Your Choices

  • Control permissions in device settings.
  • Turn notifications on or off in the app.
  • Request account deletion in Settings.

Contact

For questions or requests, contact us at support@territoryfitness.com.
